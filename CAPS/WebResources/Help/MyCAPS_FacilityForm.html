What's the difference between a closed and permanently closed facility?

  • A closed facility is one that can be re-opened. It is still a record that is editable. This is to allow enrolment projections and changes to be made to the facility record in order to prepare it for re-opening.

  • A permanently closed facility is one that cannot be re-opened. It is a record that is not editable. The facility has physically been torn down, removed, etc., and there is no ability to re-open the facility anymore.

Is there a way to see changes that have been made to this facility record? Two methods:

  1. Select the Related tab and find "Audit History". This will give you a list of changes.

  2. Select the related tab and find Facility Histories. These are records snapshotted due to a change or at yearly rollover.
