Portables Landing Page

A list of Portables will be shown here. To narrow down the list of Portables, you can can change the view (e.g. Active Portables, Inactive Portables) and/or input filters by clicking on the columns (e.g. only show Portables in the school district Richmond).

How do I filter out Modulars on this view?

  • Modulars are not including in portable records. Navigate to the desired facility record and you will see the "Number of Modulars" field.
